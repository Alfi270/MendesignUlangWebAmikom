Program Overview

International Relations is an academic discipline that seeks to understand and analyze the practice of interaction among international actors in the world system.

Our program focuses on two main areas of study, or subfields both traditional and non-traditional International Relations:

  1. The study of International Business Diplomacy based on the development of social entrepreneur which related to the political economy of globalization.
  2. The Study of Global Security focus on war, peace, cooperation, conflict resolution, international institutions and organizations, norms and international law and also the emerging of power and justice in global issues such as gender, human rights, environmental politics, and social justice.

We broadly utilize various approach from Social Science, Political Economy and International Relations to help student enable understand and analyze the problems which emerge in international affairs and also put it on a practical level to achieve peace and prosperity.

Vision & Mission

Visi

Menjadi unggul dalam penyelenggaran pendidikan, penelitian dan pengabdian masyarakat dalam disiplin ilmu hubungan internasional dengan kompetensi diplomasi bisnis internasional berbasis sociopreneurship dan keamanan global pada tahun 2031.

Misi

Melaksanakan pendidikan dan pengajaran yang dapat menghasilkan lulusan sesuai dengan kompetensi bidang diplomasi bisnis dan keamanan internasional. Melaksanakan penelitian yang berkontribusi terhadap pemutakhiran keilmuan dan perumusan kebijakan dalam konteks diplomasi bisnis dan keamanan internasional. Melaksanakan pengabdian masyarakat yang relevan dengan kebutuhan masyarakat lokal dan global dalam lingkup hubungan internasional yang relevan dengan pengembangan sociopreneurship dan ekonomi kreatif.
